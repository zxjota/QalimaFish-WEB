Welcome to MEXCNIA ("we", "us", or "our"). MEXCNIA is a catch-recording mobile application that helps anglers log their catches, photos, locations and fishing notes. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding your information when you use the MEXCNIA app (the "Service").

We collect information to provide, maintain, and improve the Service. The types of information we collect include:

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or contact us, we may collect:

  • Full name (optional), username
  • Email address (required for account & contact), e.g. charleswesonga98@gmail.com
  • Password (hashed and stored securely)

1.2 User Content

Content you upload or create within the app:

  • Catch records (species, weight, length, bait, notes)

We automatically collect certain technical information about your device and how you use the Service, such as:
